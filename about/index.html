A Little Bit About Me....

I live in Butte, MT with my family and small herd of livestock. I started my career in public administration, policy & advocacy. After taking time to raise our daughters, I knew going back to the "work force" needed to look differently this time around. After checking out some free coding bootcamps, I felt like a place in tech might just be a good pivot. Fortunately, I found an amazing online program through Skillcrush.

As I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that will give me the freedom & versatility I am looking for in my work. All the experience I gained in my years of public policy I'm continuing to use as I forge a new path in tech. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development and look forward to completing my coursework in late summer 2022. As I progress through the FE course, I'll continue to update my portfolio with all my successes.
